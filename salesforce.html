My Salesforce Adventure

Why Salesforce/CRM?
I was introduced to Trailhead in June 2022.
It seemed like a natural progression as Medicine also has so many elements related to Customer(patient) relationship management.
You are also selling your brand as a doctor, and have to look at potential patients as leads, bring them in as opportunities and then deal with issues and cases as a result of your treatment.
The future of medicine is in the cloud , is a firm belief of mine!!(I have even had the opportunity to even contribute to our provincial E-Health Initiative).
By combining my Salesforce Learning and basic programming knowledge, I have also improved knowledge access and dissemination in my department at the hospital.

Goals
I must admit, I found it very challenging initially to understand business processes and thinking
But after 6 months I really started to grasp the essence of it and despite failing my first exam , I passed the administrator exam in February 2023.
I combined this with understanding basic programming, and started using
Free code camp
The Odin Project
and The Learn enough series
to become adept at basic front end development
I am by no means an expert but have a good grasp of the concepts and these made my salesforce journey easier, adding context to my learning of the platform
Using the courses provided by Mike Wheeler has also vastly improve my understanding ( I have even completed the career launch accelerator programme)
Currently I am studying towards my Platform App Builder Certification and hope to write (and pass!!) during 2023
I am in the process of completing various superbadges on the platform as well, and am hoping to gain experience via Part-time work.
I am hoping to ,mainly, study the full gambit of admin certifications over the next 12 months, and even the web-developer certifications as my programming knowledge grows.
My hope is to combine the medical and CRM/programming spheres of my knowledge to provide a holistic approach to health data management for potential employers.

The Future
I am currently working on a CRM style emergency department app for use by doctors to decrease errors via data validation and on-page guidance for abnormal findings and values.
Although in its infancy, it is already shaping up to be a valuable tool, that I may bring to market if fully functional
