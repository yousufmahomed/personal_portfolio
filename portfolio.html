Accident & Emergency Department Learning Group website

This website was developed to assist the doctors under me with accessing the tutorials I had developed and presented to them.

It categorizes the tutorials into relevant sections and includes our twitter feed and relevant documents they will need while working under me.

It currently has a membership of over 400 doctors and students from all around the globe and is constantly growing.

It is also a wonderful place to view my public speaking, leadership, and training capabilities.

Emergency Department Assistant website

This website was developed to assist new doctors and students who may be exposed to an emergency department for the first time

It provides guides to most aspects of seeing a patient in an emergency area, along with more complicated calculators to assist in diagnosis

It is a large project that is still being expanded, but is in use by the same cohort of doctors as for the learning group
